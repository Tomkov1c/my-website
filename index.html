So in my 3rd year of highschool, I constatntly had to carry some paper database idagrams to my class. I used a latop to take notes and after sometime it became anoying to carry that one specific piece of paper around, so it thought "Why not just digitize it?", so I did. It accured to me that other students may have the same problem.
This is the result. Lapschool is just a place where you can find a lot of diagrams,tools and more digitalized and interactible on a computer so you wont have to carry anything with you to classes, just your laptop.

The Periodic Table

This is currently the only ported "tool". It has everything that a regular paper periodic table would have but I added a feature a new feature. You can click on the element and the site will display some addititional information. I am no chemist and all data is used from Wikipedia, so please report any mistakes you find.

This project is still in the early deverlopment. Any updates are probbably gonna be documented on this page. If you like the idea of this project, please consider contacting me and let me know if you have any ideas on what to add to the site.
