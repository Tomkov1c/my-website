    Programming is a challenging field to enter. I couldn't find any excellent tutorials for beginners,
    so I started writing in Microsoft Word, aiming for an explanation that a 7th grader could understand.
</p>
<h2>Why?</h2>
<p class="sectionProjectsProjectParagraph">
    I often watch well-edited and well-written content that is pleasant to watch, like 
    <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@T2norway" target="_blank">T2norway's videos</a>. I aspired to create something
    as awesome as his videos. Considering the things I could make a video on in that style, I realized I could explain
    the basics of programming in a straightforward way. So, I started working on a script, and voila.
</p>
